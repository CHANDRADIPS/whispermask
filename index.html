💡 How It Works

MEMS microphones in smart devices rely on internal hardware pre-amplifiers. WhisperMask emits near-ultrasonic wave profiles (19 kHz) that are inaudible to human ears but force the microphone's pre-amplifier to generate physical harmonic non-linearities in the audible spectrum, creating a chaotic "energy wall" that completely masks speech parameters inside the device's voice model.
